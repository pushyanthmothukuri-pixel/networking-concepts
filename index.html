The Cosmic Wonder

The stars awake in the velvet night,

Bathing the world in silver light.

The moon ascends her silent throne,

A glowing pearl in the vast unknown.

Planets dance in a cosmic ring,

To a quiet song that the comets sing.

Galaxies spin in the dark above,

A boundless ocean of peace and love.

We gaze up high from our earthly place,

Lost in the wonder of endless space.
